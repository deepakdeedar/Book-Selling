Description
Digital Electronics: Principles And Integrated Circuits is an all-inclusive book on the core concepts of digital electronics and integrated circuits.
Summary Of The Book
One needs to get a good grasp of the basics of digital electronics to comprehend the design and working of consumer/industrial electronics, embedded systems, communications, computers, and security equipment. The devices that are used in such applications are continuously improving in size and technology. Dr. Anil K. Maini’s Digital Electronics: Principles and Integrated Circuits helps students and engineers get a better understanding of the basics and the implementation and application principles of digital electronics and integrated circuits.
Digital Electronics: Principles and Integrated Circuits is a precise book that provides information on fundamental theory, operational aspects and potential applications. Unlike other books, this one is a complete book that is fully illustrated. Designed for undergraduate and graduate students, the book provides comprehensive coverage on Boolean algebra and simplification techniques, binary codes, number systems, logic gates and families, and digital arithmetic.
Various latest application fields including digital instrumentation, programmable logic devices, microcontrollers, microprocessors, and digital troubleshooting are explained here. The other topics covered by Maini in this book are combinational logic circuits, memory devices, and arithmetic circuits.
Digital Electronics: Principles and Integrated Circuits includes an in-depth coverage of data conversion circuits, devices for arithmetic operations, multiplexers, flip-flops and related devices, counters and registers, and de-multiplexers. The book is sufficiently illustrated as each chapter comes loaded with design examples and diagrams to ensure easy understanding for the students. Also, at the end of each chapter, self-evaluation exercises are provided. These include solved objective type questions and multiple-choice questions.
